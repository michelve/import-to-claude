# How to Transfer Your Context from ChatGPT to Claude

Move your AI memory and preferences from ChatGPT (or Gemini) to Claude in a few simple steps.

---

## Step 1 - Start the import on Claude.ai

1. Go to [Claude.ai](https://claude.ai) and open **Settings**.
2. Select **Capabilities** from the sidebar.
3. Under the **"Import memory from other AI providers"** section, click **Start import**.

A pop-up will appear with a prewritten prompt to copy.

---

## Step 2 - Paste the prompt into ChatGPT (or Gemini)

Copy the prompt provided by Claude and paste it into a new chat on the platform you are migrating from (e.g. ChatGPT or Gemini).

The prompt is also available here: [IMPORT_PROMPT.md](./IMPORT_PROMPT.md)

---

## Step 3 - Copy the response

The AI will reply with a structured summary of what it knows about you - broken down into sections such as identity, career, and projects - along with notes on how you prefer your AI to communicate (tone of voice, style, etc.).

Wait for the full response to generate, then copy it.

---

## Step 4 - Add the context to Claude

1. Return to the Claude settings pop-up.
2. Paste the copied response into the text box.
3. Click **Add to memory**.

A **"Manage memory"** dialog will appear showing all the personal context Claude now holds. After a minute or two, it will update with the newly imported data.

> **Tip:** Review the imported context carefully and remove or correct anything that is inaccurate or no longer relevant.

---

You are all set - Claude is now ready with your context.
